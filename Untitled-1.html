AESPA

æspa is a popular K-pop girl group from SM Entertainment known for its unique "metaverse" concept and blend of digital and real-world elements. The name "æspa" combines the English initialism for "Avatar X Experience" with the word "aspect," representing the idea of meeting another self in a new world. The group's digital avatars, known as "æ-members," exist in a virtual dimension called the FLAT. Since their debut in 2020 with the digital single "Black Mamba", the four members—Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning—have released a series of successful albums and singles. Their discography includes the mini-albums Savage (2021) and Girls (2022), and their first full-length studio album, Armageddon, was released in May 2024. æspa has been recognized with numerous awards for their music, including winning Song of the Year (Daesang) for "Supernova" at the 2024 MAMA Awards and multiple Daesangs at the 2024 Melon Music Awards. The group has also performed at major international events like Coachella and the UN High-level Political Forum on Sustainable Development. They are currently represented by SM Entertainment, Warner Records, and Capitol Records.
